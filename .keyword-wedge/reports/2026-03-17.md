## Executive Summary

QC currently has zero SEO surface area — only 5 indexable pages, no content pages, no blog. The trust-builder audit proposed 13 features to give visitors free value before signup. This keyword analysis validates which of those features would actually attract organic traffic.

**Top finding:** Love language quizzes are QC's #1 SEO opportunity, with 200k–1m+ combined monthly searches and multiple Low competition entry points. This was undervalued in the original trust-builder audit, which prioritized the Interactive Demo (a feature with minimal search demand).

## Revised Feature Prioritization

### Priority 1: Free Love Language Quiz (`/tools/love-language-quiz`)

**Keyword opportunity:** 200k–1m+ combined monthly searches
**Entry point:** `5 love languages quiz` (10k–100k, Low competition)
**Why #1:** Largest volume with achievable competition. QC already has love language infrastructure. Free web-based quiz with partner comparison differentiates vs. official quiz (which requires email, no comparison).
**Funnel:** Quiz → partner comparison → "Save & track over time" → signup

### Priority 2: Conversation Starters (`/tools/conversation-starters`)

**Keyword opportunity:** 1k–10k for primary term, plus long-tail variants
**Entry point:** `couples check in questions` (100–1k, Low competition)
**Why #2:** Low-effort content play. Web-based instant access differentiates vs. Gottman Card Decks app.
**Funnel:** Browse prompts → "Want structured sessions?" → demo → signup

### Priority 3: Relationship Assessment Quiz (`/tools/relationship-quiz`)

**Keyword opportunity:** 1k–10k combined across quiz variants
**Entry point:** `couples quiz` (1k–10k, Low competition)
**Why #3:** Low competition entry, builds quiz infrastructure reusable for other assessments.
**Funnel:** Assessment → partner comparison → "Track growth" → signup

### Priority 4: Interactive Demo Check-In (`/demo/checkin`)

**Keyword opportunity:** 100–1k (minimal)
**Why demoted:** The demo is valuable as a conversion tool once visitors arrive, but won't drive organic traffic. Related keywords have tiny volume.
**Role:** Conversion optimization, not traffic acquisition.

### Priority 5: Communication Exercises Content

**Keyword opportunity:** 1k–10k across informational intent keywords
**Why last:** High competition, requires ongoing content creation. Better as a long-term SEO play after tools are established.

## SEO Infrastructure Gaps

Before any trust-builder tool can rank, fix these foundational issues:

1. **Add canonical URLs** to root metadata
2. **Implement JSON-LD** (Organization schema, FAQ schema on tool pages)
3. **Consider SSR for landing page** — all content is currently client-rendered via `'use client'`
4. **Add favicon.ico** to public/
5. **Expand sitemap** to include `/tools/*` routes once built
6. **Add `/tools/*` to public routes** in middleware
